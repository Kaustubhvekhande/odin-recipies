Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken biryani is a popular dish in India. It is made with rice and chicken, and is often served with a side of raita (a yogurt-based sauce). The dish is usually cooked in a pot or pan, and can be served either hot or cold.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup chicken breast, cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup green pepper, chopped

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice
  2. Cook the rice
  3. Marinate the chicken
  4. Fry the onions
  5. Cook the chicken
  6. Layer the biryani
  7. repeat the layers
  8. Dum cooking
